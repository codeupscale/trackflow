# TrackFlow

### Time Tracking. Activity Monitoring. HR Management. One Platform.

---

## The Problem

Growing distributed teams use 4-6 separate tools for workforce management. Time tracking in one app. Leave management in spreadsheets. Attendance in a biometric system. Payroll reconciled manually in Excel. Data is duplicated, delayed, and wrong.

## The Solution

TrackFlow replaces them all with a single platform where time entries feed attendance records, leave balances update automatically, and payroll calculates from real data instead of spreadsheets.

---

## Three Reasons Teams Switch to TrackFlow

**1. One Platform, Not Six**
Time tracking, activity monitoring, screenshots, leave management, attendance, shift scheduling, payroll, and employee records. All connected. No reconciliation.

**2. Data You Can Trust**
Local-first architecture means no data is lost when the WiFi drops. Per-display multi-monitor screenshots show what actually happened. Activity scoring matches industry-standard models.

**3. Self-Service That Actually Works**
Employees check leave balances, view payslips, submit corrections, and manage documents without emailing HR. Managers approve requests, review timesheets, and monitor activity from one dashboard.
