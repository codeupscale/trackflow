## Production Notes

**Total duration**: 77 seconds

**Narration style**: Calm, measured, authoritative. Male or female voice. Not rushed. Let the visuals carry the detail; the narration carries the story.

**Transitions**: Clean dissolves between scenes. No flashy effects. The product speaks for itself.

**Screen recordings**: Use real product UI for all dashboard and desktop agent shots. Do not use mockups. Real data with anonymized names.

**Music**: Fade in at 0:00, maintain at low volume under narration, slight build at 1:00 (Why TrackFlow), resolve at CTA.

**Color palette**: Match TrackFlow brand. Warm amber-orange accents on a clean white/light gray background.

**Resolution**: 1920x1080 for web. 1080x1080 square cut for social media.

**Captions**: Hardcoded English captions throughout for silent autoplay on social.
